The Problem — A Broken System on Both Ends
Global companies struggle to pay African talent. African workers struggle to receive — and survive between — paydays.
1
Fragmented Banking Rails & Compliance Chaos
International companies must navigate disconnected banking systems, differing tax codes per country, and manual statutory remittances — creating compliance risk and costly operations overhead.
2
Multi-Day Settlement Delays
Workers wait 3–7 business days for salaries to clear. In high-inflation markets, every day of delay is real purchasing power lost.
3
Zero Financial Safety Nets
African remote workers and contractors have no employer-backed emergency credit access. Medical emergencies, equipment failures, or gaps between contracts leave them financially exposed with no recourse.

Solvo is a compliance-first, automated global payroll engine built on Kora's bulk payout and multi-currency infrastructure. International companies log in once to onboard their African workforce, configure pay schedules, and let Solvo handle everything else — multi-currency salary disbursement, real-time local tax remittances to each jurisdiction's revenue authority, and on-demand emergency advance credit to workers — all landing directly in local bank accounts or mobile wallets within seconds.

How It Works
01
Company Onboarding International client connects via Solvo's dashboard, adds African team members, and configures multi-currency pay schedules and compliance rules per country.
02
Bulk Multi-Currency Disbursement Kora's bulk payout engine simultaneously processes salary payments across multiple African markets — bypassing correspondent banking delays.
03
Automated Statutory Remittance Solvo calculates, deducts, and remits PAYE, pension contributions, and other local taxes to the correct revenue authority — automatically, on time.
04
Instant Local Settlement Net salary lands in the worker's local bank account or mobile wallet within seconds. Emergency advances available on-demand via the worker portal.
